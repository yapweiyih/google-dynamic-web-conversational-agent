Your Travel Class Determines Your Allowance

The amount of baggage you can check in for free depends on your travel class and fare type. Here's a general overview:

Travel Class Checked Baggage Allowance
Suite / First Class 50kg
Business Class 40kg
Premium Economy Class 35kg
Economy Class 30kg

Note: These are standard allowances. The exact allowance may vary depending on your specific fare type and route. Always check your ticket for details.

Carry-on Baggage

You are allowed to bring one cabin bag and one personal item on board. The size and weight limits are:

  • Cabin bag: Maximum 55cm x 38cm x 23cm (total dimensions must not exceed 115cm)
  • Personal item: Maximum 40cm x 30cm x 10cm (e.g., handbag, laptop bag, small backpack)
  • Combined weight: Must not exceed 7kg

Additional Baggage and Overweight Charges

If you need to carry more than your free baggage allowance, you can purchase additional baggage or pay overweight charges. Fees vary depending on your route and destination.

Restricted Items

For safety reasons, certain items are restricted or prohibited in both checked and carry-on baggage. These include:

  • Explosives, firearms, and weapons
  • Flammable liquids and gases
  • Corrosive materials
  • Sharp objects

Tips for a Smooth Journey

  • Check your baggage allowance before you travel.
  • Weigh your bags at home to avoid surprises at the airport.
  • Pack essential items in your carry-on bag in case your checked baggage is delayed.
  • Label your bags clearly with your name and contact information.
